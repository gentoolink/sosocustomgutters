Gutter & Eavestrough Cleaning

Kelowna's Okanagan climate — pine needles, aspen leaves, spring blooms, and fall litter — puts heavy demands on eavestrough. Clogged gutters lead to water overflow, foundation erosion, and fascia damage. We clean them out properly.

Why Clean Gutters Regularly?

After Kelowna's dry summers, the first heavy rain can send litres of water cascading off a clogged eavestrough system straight to your foundation. Spring cleaning prevents this. So does an autumn clean after the leaves drop. If you have pine or aspen trees anywhere near your roofline, twice a year is the minimum.

What Our Cleaning Includes

  • Full removal of leaves, needles, seed pods, and debris from all eavestrough runs
  • Downspout clearing — we flush and unplug even buried drain lines
  • Inspection of gutter brackets, hangers, and seals for wear
  • Minor adjustments to restore proper slope and drainage
  • Before and after photo documentation on request

Signs Your Gutters Need Cleaning

  • Water overflowing over the edge during rain — not just the corners
  • Plants or weeds growing in the eavestrough
  • Sagging or pulling away from the fascia
  • Staining on the siding below the gutter line
  • Basement dampness after heavy rain
  • Pine needles or leaf buildup visible from the ground
  • Birds or wasps nesting in the gutter
  • Ice dams forming early in winter
