Property Types We Service

Commercial properties have different demands than residential — larger roof areas, complex drainage requirements, and no tolerance for leaks that damage inventory or infrastructure. We have the equipment and experience to handle multi-storey and large-footprint buildings.

  • Retail plazas and strip malls — Harvey Avenue, Bernard Avenue, Main Street Kelowna
  • Office buildings and medical clinics
  • Industrial warehouses and manufacturing facilities
  • Multi-family residential — apartment blocks and townhouse complexes
  • Hotels, motels, and hospitality properties
  • Schools, community centres, and municipal buildings

Common Commercial Gutter Problems

  • Large roof areas generating high water volume — standard gutters overflow
  • Flat or low-slope roofscapes requiring internal drains and scuppers
  • Strata corporations managing multiple buildings with aging systems
  • Ice buildup on elevated walkways and entrances
  • Tree debris from mature street trees — poplars, birches, and pines
  • Leaking causing interior ceiling damage and liability concerns
  • Deferred maintenance from previous property management companies
  • Drain lines running underground that are difficult to clear
