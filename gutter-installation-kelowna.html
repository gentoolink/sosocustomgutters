Professional Gutter Installation in Kelowna

From Dilworth Mountain to Black Mountain, we install continuous eavestrough systems that last.

If your gutters are sagging, leaking, or more than 15 years old, it's time for replacement. In Kelowna's dry summers and wet springs, working eavestrough is the difference between a dry foundation and an expensive repair bill.

Why Continuous Eavestrough?

Traditional sectional gutters leak at the seams. Continuous eavestrough — also called seamless gutters — is measured and formed on-site to fit your exact roofline. No seams means no leaks, no rust, and less maintenance year after year.

What We Install

  • Continuous aluminum eavestrough — 5" and 6" widths
  • Downspouts placed for optimal water diversion
  • Custom bends and extensions for complex rooflines
  • New fascia boards and hangers where needed
  • Leaf gutter guards pre-installed on request
