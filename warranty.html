Our Workmanship Warranty

Every eavestrough system we install is backed by a 10-year workmanship warranty. This covers the installation itself — proper slope toward downspouts, sealed corners, secure mounting, and correct extension positioning. If your gutters sag, if a joint leaks that shouldn't, if a downspout wasn't properly secured — we come back and fix it at no charge within the warranty period.

We put this in writing because we know the gutter industry has a trust problem. Everyone claims they're different. We think the proof is in what happens when something goes wrong — and we have a track record of showing up.

What the Workmanship Warranty Covers

  • Proper slope and drainage — water flows to downspouts as designed
  • Sealed mitered corners and end caps — no leaking at joints
  • Secure mounting — no sagging, no pulling away from fascia
  • Correct downspout extension — water directed 4+ feet from foundation
  • Structural integrity of the installation under normal load conditions

Material Warranties

We use aluminum from established suppliers with documented material warranties. The aluminum itself carries a 25-year material warranty — this is passed through from the manufacturer and covers defects in the metal (not damage from impact, improper cleaning, or acts of nature). We provide the manufacturer warranty documentation for every installation.

For the hardware we install — hangers, brackets, screws, sealants — we use products from Alu-Rex and equivalent manufacturers that carry their own warranties. The Alu-Rex T-Rex continuous hanger system is backed by a 25-year limited warranty from Alu-Rex on the hangers themselves.

What We Use and Why

We don't cut corners on materials because cheap materials cost more in callbacks than they save in initial price. Here is what we install and why we choose each product:

0.027-Gauge Aluminum (Standard Residential)

Heavy enough for the Okanagan's freeze-thaw climate without being so heavy that it stresses your fascia. Thinner aluminum (0.024 gauge or less) dents easily, deforms under snow load, and doesn't hold its shape through a Okanagan winter. We use 0.027 minimum for all residential installations. For commercial or agricultural properties with high snow loads, we use 0.032-gauge aluminum.

Alu-Rex T-Rex Continuous Hangers

T-Rex hangers are a continuous bar system — not individual spikes driven through the gutter into the fascia. They distribute weight along the full length of the gutter rather than at individual points, which means the gutter cannot sag between fasteners. This is the single biggest improvement in gutter technology in the past 30 years, and it's what we install as standard on every seamless eavestrough system.

Aluminum-Compatible Sealants

We use butyl-based sealants that remain flexible through freeze-thaw cycles — not standard silicone. Silicone degrades in Okanagan UV conditions and becomes brittle within 2-3 years. Butyl stays flexible for much longer and bonds properly to aluminum surfaces.
